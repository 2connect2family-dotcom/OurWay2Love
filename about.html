About the Family

Our Story

Every family has a story — a tapestry woven from the threads of different lives, different places, and different times. Ours is no different, and to us family is more than a blood connection. We are a family shaped by love, support, laughter, hard work, and a deep commitment to one another through every season of life.

Our family roots began in Virginia and South Carolina and have since spread far and wide across regions while keeping us united in spirit, tradition, and love. Through every generation, the people who came before us built lives with their hands, raised children with their hearts, and left legacies defined by the strength of their character. We carry their names, their values, and their spirit forward every day.

Who We Are

We are parents and grandparents, siblings and cousins, aunts and uncles and the beloved friends who became family along the way. We span generations and places, but we are united by a bond that distance and time cannot diminish.

We celebrate our differences and cherish our shared traditions — from holiday gatherings that fill the house with warmth, to quiet moments of connection that remind us what matters most.

Our family has always believed in welcoming others with open arms and open hearts. Whether greeting relatives, friends, or new acquaintances, we strive to make everyone feel valued, loved, and at home. One of the greatest gifts we share is unconditional understanding and the comfort of knowing that each person matters.

Our Values

Faith, family, and service guide us. We believe in showing up for each other — in the big moments and the small ones. We believe in passing down wisdom, stories, and traditions so that those who come after us know where they came from and feel proud of it.
